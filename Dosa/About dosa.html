Dosa

Dosa is a popular South Indian dish made from fermented rice and urad dal (black gram) batter. It is a thin, crispy pancake that is typically served with various chutneys and sambar. Dosas can be plain or stuffed with fillings like spiced potatoes (masala dosa).

Idli

Idli is a traditional South Indian steamed cake made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. It is soft, fluffy, and usually served as a breakfast item along with chutneys and sambar. Idlis are known for being light and healthy, making them a popular choice for all age groups.
